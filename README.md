# 📊 E-Mart Sales Dashboard

An interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built to analyze E-Mart’s sales performance using business intelligence concepts.
This project visualizes key metrics such as sales, profit, quantity, and profit margin to help understand business trends 
and support data-driven decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **E-Mart Sales Dashboard** provides a complete overview of sales performance across different **product categories, regions, 
products, and shipping types**. It highlights year-to-date performance metrics and identifies top-performing as well as 
underperforming products.

This dashboard is designed to help businesses quickly monitor performance and uncover actionable insights.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics Tracked

- **YTD Sales:** $11.53M  
- **YTD Profit:** $1.34M  
- **YTD Quantity Sold:** 107K  
- **Profit Margin:** 11.58%

The dashboard also compares **Year-over-Year (YoY) performance** to evaluate growth or decline.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales by Category
- Compares sales performance for:
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies
  - Technology
- Includes YoY trend analysis.

### 2️⃣ Top 5 Products
Identifies the highest revenue-generating products to understand best sellers.

### 3️⃣ Bottom 5 Products
Highlights low-performing products to identify improvement opportunities.

### 4️⃣ Sales by Region
Geographical visualization of sales distribution across regions using a map.

### 5️⃣ Sales by Shipping Type
Breakdown of sales based on shipping methods to understand logistics trends.

### 6️⃣ Interactive Filters
Users can filter the dashboard by **product category** to dynamically explore insights.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

## 🎯 Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- Which products are performing the best and worst?
- Which regions contribute the most to total sales?
- What is the current profit margin and sales trend?
